Having trouble opening the app? ›

macOS may show a security warning on first launch. Here's how to fix it in seconds:

  1. Open the .dmg and drag VaultWalls to your Applications folder.
  2. Double-click VaultWalls — you'll see a warning. Click Done.
  3. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security.
  4. Scroll down and click "Open Anyway".
  5. Click Open on the final prompt. You're in — this won't appear again.
